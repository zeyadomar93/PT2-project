1. Pressing the key '+' will enlarge the ball size.
2. Pressing the key '-' will shrink the ball size.
3. Pressing the key 'Y' or 'y' will turn the ball to yellow in color.
4. Pressing the key 'W' or 'w' will turn the ball to white in color.
5. Pressing the spacebar key will turn the ball to a random color.
6. Pressing an arrow key will move the ball to the corresponding direction respectively by 10 pixels. 
   For example pressing the left arrow key will move the ball to the left.
